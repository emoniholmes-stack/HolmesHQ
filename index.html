My Story

Hi, my name is Emoni. I grew up in the small town of Wadena, raised by my single mother. From an early age, I was deeply involved in the church, and my faith has been central to my life. I accepted Christ in 2014 and was baptized in 2016 at Wadena Assembly of God, a small but vibrant community where my mother serves as the youth pastor, worship leader, and also takes care of maintaining the church.

Being part of the church and volunteering alongside my mother shaped my values and work ethic. It stirred in me a passion not only for business but for making God the center of everything I do. These experiences continue to inspire me to pursue my goals with purpose, faith, and integrity.

Education

I graduated from Wadena Deer Creek High School in Wadena, MN, in May 2023. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration at Trinity Bible College and Graduate School, where I began my studies in August 2023 and plan to graduate in May 2027. My studies focus on combining practical business skills with a faith-driven approach, preparing me to lead with integrity and purpose in the professional world.
