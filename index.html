My Story

Hi, my name is Emoni. I grew up in the small town of Wadena, raised by my single mother. From an early age, I was deeply involved in the church, and my faith has been central to my life. I accepted Christ in 2014 and was baptized in 2016 at Wadena Assembly of God, a small but vibrant community where my mother serves as the youth pastor, worship leader, and also takes care of maintaining the church.

Being part of the church and volunteering alongside my mother shaped my values and work ethic. It stirred in me a passion not only for business but for making God the center of everything I do. These experiences continue to inspire me to pursue my goals with purpose, faith, and integrity.

Education

I graduated from Wadena Deer Creek High School in Wadena, MN, in May 2023. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration at Trinity Bible College and Graduate School, where I began my studies in August 2023 and plan to graduate in May 2027.

Experience

I have gained diverse experience in customer service and administrative roles. I work as a Front Desk Receptionist at AmericInn Hotel and Suites in Wadena, MN, where I assist guests with check-ins and introduce them to hotel amenities.

Ethics Check #4

For my site, I plan to respect ownership by only using content I created or content I have permission to use. If I use someone else’s work, I’ll credit them clearly and follow the licensing rules they set. A Creative Commons license I’d use is CC BY because it lets others use my work as long as they give proper credit, which keeps it open but respectful. The Bible tells us in Romans 13:7a to “Give to everyone what you owe them,” and that includes honoring people for their ideas and creativity.
